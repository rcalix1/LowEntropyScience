what is entropy?

entropy is the quiet decay of systems over time. your tools rust. your body gets tired. your files go missing. your friendships fade. entropy is everywhere — but it doesn’t have to win.

what we do here

the low-entropy detective investigates these subtle breakdowns. we name them. we track them. we fight back. sometimes with rust remover. sometimes with a notebook. sometimes with a better chair.
